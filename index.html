Case 01

Scripties

2025

A speech writing and rehearsal tool built to make public speaking feel measurable instead of vague. Scripties combines structure, pacing awareness, and a calmer writing flow into one focused experience.

Product + UI Swift Accelerator 2025 Speech practice
Open project
What it solves Rehearsal usually lacks feedback

Scripties turns pacing and delivery into something students can actually inspect and improve.

Why it matters Confidence grows when the process is visible

The product reduces uncertainty by making preparation feel concrete.

Case 02

Flowday

2025

A student planning system that brings goals, exam dates, and focus sessions into one place. The idea is straightforward: fewer scattered tools, stronger study rhythm.

Student systems Web product Focus design
Open project
What it solves Students switch context too often

Flowday creates one control surface for planning, deadlines, and daily focus.

Design lens Keep the interface calmer than the schedule

Structure and visual restraint help students think about the work, not the tool.

Case 04

BuildingBlocs

2025

A machine learning project that translates sign language into letters to support more accessible communication. It started from a simple question: how can computer vision lower friction for people who are often overlooked?

Accessibility Machine learning Computer vision
What it solves Accessibility should not feel like an afterthought

The project explores how vision models can translate intent into a more usable interaction.

Build principle Meaningful tech starts with empathy

The strongest technical decisions came from focusing on real communication barriers.
